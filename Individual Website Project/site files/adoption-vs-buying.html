Should you adopt or buy?

Both dog adoption and buying have their pros and cons.
Adopting is an opportunity to save a dog in need, while Buying gives you more control over specific traits and health.

Benefits of Dog Adoption:

Saving a Life

Less Expensive

Variety of Dogs

Supporting Rescue Organizations

Challenges of Dog Adoption:

Unknown History

Less Control Over Specific Traits





Benefits of Buying a dog:

Puppy Bonding

Predictable Health

Specific Needs

Control Over Traits

Challenges of Buying a dog:

Higher Cost

Ethical Concerns

Potential for Puppy Mills
